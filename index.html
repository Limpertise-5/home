Fim Scanner

Our Freshly designed Fingerprint attendance checker utilizing the fingerprint scanner available on most modern smartphones.

How does it work ?

Limpertise's Fingerprint scanner will scan for attendee's fingerprint and input it into the server's database. The attendance data will then be processed throughout custom programs we will provide to our customers.
